Thinking about making your first chess game? Look no further with the Pixel Art Chess Asset Pack.

This complete Pixel Art Chess Asset Pack (and growing) contains the entire set of  game assets including two background boards, text and numerical. The assets are set up as individual sprites, and sprite texture atlas. The assets are organized, named logically, trimmed to the smallest size. They can be used for non-commercial and commercial games
